❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Answers to your most common questions.

Am I eligible to rent a vehicle on PrimeDrew in India?

Yes, provided you are **18 years or older** and possess a **valid driving license**. You must upload proof during registration for verification purposes.

How much is the refundable Security Deposit?

The deposit varies based on your booking duration, ranging from **₹500** for rentals under 24 hours to a maximum of **₹5000** for longer rentals. It is fully refunded once the vehicle is returned safely and on time.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your booking within **1 hour** of payment without any charge. After 1 hour, a **10% cancellation fee** of the total booking amount will be charged, and the remainder will be refunded.

How much commission do I earn as a Host?

Hosts default to earning **70%** of the rental value (excluding the security deposit). Experienced hosts who qualify for the Premium Tier can earn up to **80%**.

What happens if I return the vehicle late?

A late return may incur an additional hourly fee, which can be higher than the original rate. Please contact the Host immediately if you know you will be delayed to avoid unnecessary penalties.
